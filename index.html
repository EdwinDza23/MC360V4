FAQ

Common Questions

Can I use multiple Maritime Connect products together? ▾
Yes. All Maritime Connect products share a single platform with unified login and user management. You can adopt one product and add others at any time — your team and vessel data carries across all products without re-entry.
Does the Bunker Quantity Survey product work offline? ▾
Yes. The mobile application for surveyors supports offline data capture — measurements, photos, and notes are stored locally and synced automatically when connectivity is restored. This is designed specifically for working on vessels where connectivity is limited.
Which sanctions lists does the Global Sanctions product screen against? ▾
The Global Sanctions product screens against OFAC (US), the EU Consolidated Sanctions List, UN Security Council Consolidated List, and HM Treasury (UK). The lists are updated automatically, and you can configure alerts for any newly matched entities.
Can Maritime Connect generate branded PDF reports? ▾
Yes. All survey and inspection products generate PDF reports with your company logo, header, and footer. Report templates can be configured by your administrator. Reports are generated automatically on survey or inspection completion.
How long does implementation take? ▾
Most teams are operational within 5 to 10 business days. This includes platform configuration, user onboarding, and an initial training session. Complex multi-product implementations with API integrations may take longer — your implementation specialist will scope this during the onboarding call.
Do you offer data migration from our existing systems? ▾
Yes. Our implementation team can assist with importing historical vessel records, survey data, and user accounts. We support CSV import and API-based migration for structured data. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific migration requirements.
Where is our data stored? ▾
Data is stored in ISO 27001-certified cloud infrastructure with options for regional data residency in Europe, Asia-Pacific, and North America. Data residency requirements can be specified during your implementation setup.
Does Maritime Connect support SSO and SAML? ▾
Yes. Enterprise plans include support for SAML 2.0-based SSO integration with identity providers including Microsoft Azure AD, Okta, and Google Workspace. Contact our sales team to discuss your organization's identity requirements.
Is pricing per user or per vessel? ▾
Pricing depends on the product and your organization's size. Some products are priced per active user, others include vessel-based licensing for fleet management features. Request a pricing proposal from our sales team for a quote specific to your requirements.
Are there volume discounts for larger fleets? ▾
Yes. Organizations managing 20+ vessels or with 50+ users qualify for volume pricing. Enterprise multi-year agreements also include additional discounts. Speak to our sales team for a custom proposal.
What support is included in the subscription? ▾
All plans include email support with a 24-hour response SLA, a knowledge base with product documentation, and quarterly check-in calls with your customer success manager. Enterprise plans include priority support with a 4-hour response SLA and a dedicated success manager.
Is support available across time zones? ▾
Yes. Our support team covers Asia-Pacific, Europe, and Americas time zones. Maritime operations don't stop, so we make sure critical support requests receive a response regardless of when they're submitted.

Product Overview

What Is Bunker Quantity Survey?

The Bunker Quantity Survey product digitizes the complete process of independently verifying the quantity of fuel delivered to a vessel. Marine surveyors use the mobile app to capture tank measurements, record temperatures, and document the delivery on board — then the system automatically calculates the received quantity using international standards.

The final report is generated automatically and delivered to the client within minutes of the survey being completed — with all measurements, calculations, and photographic evidence attached.

  • Used by marine survey companies, ship owners, and fuel suppliers
  • Works on iOS and Android, with full offline support
  • Complies with ASTM/IP measurement standards
  • Auto-generates branded PDF reports on completion

What Bunker Survey Eliminates

🧮

Manual Calculations

Quantity calculations from tank tables, trim corrections, and temperature adjustments are complex and error-prone when done manually. The product automates every calculation to international standards.

Fuel Quantity Disputes

Without independent digital records, disputes between ship owners and fuel suppliers have no neutral evidence base. Every measurement and calculation is timestamped and locked on completion.

Delayed Reporting

Manually assembling a survey report in Word takes hours. With Maritime Connect, the report is generated automatically the moment the survey is marked complete by the surveyor.

Documentation Errors

🔌

Disconnected Workflows

No Operational Visibility

Survey managers have no real-time view of field activity. The dashboard shows every active survey, its current status, and any open issues — so nothing falls through the cracks.

Features

Everything the Survey Process Needs

Digital Survey Workflow

Replace paper checklists with a structured digital survey form that guides surveyors through every step of the bunker quantity survey process — vessel details, tank selection, measurement capture, and sign-off.

  • Configurable survey templates for HFO, MGO, and LNG
  • Step-by-step guided workflow for junior surveyors
  • Mandatory field validation to prevent incomplete submissions
  • Offline data capture that syncs when connectivity returns
📋

Evidence Capture

Every survey includes a complete evidence trail — tank measurement photos, loading arm seals, delivery receipts, and ullage reports are captured in-app and attached to the final report automatically.

  • In-app camera with automatic geotagging and timestamp
  • Document scanning for BDN and supplier receipts
  • Evidence organized by survey section automatically
  • Secure, tamper-evident photo storage
📸

Calculation Engine

The built-in calculation engine applies ASTM/IP standard correction factors for temperature, trim, and list — automatically converting observed tank readings to corrected volume and mass quantities.

  • ASTM volume correction factor tables built in
  • Trim and list correction calculations
  • Multiple grade calculations in a single survey
  • All calculations auditable and locked after submission
🧮

Automated Reports

Branded PDF reports are generated automatically when the survey is submitted. Reports include all measurements, calculations, evidence photos, and the surveyor's declaration — formatted to industry-standard layout.

  • Branded with your company logo and contact details
  • Delivered via secure shareable link within minutes
  • Exportable to PDF or sent directly to client email
  • All reports stored and searchable in your account history
